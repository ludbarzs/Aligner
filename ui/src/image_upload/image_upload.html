Image Capture Guidelines

For best results, follow these guidelines when taking your photo:

Camera Setup

  • Avoid wide-angle lenses - Use standard lens to minimize distortion
  • Position directly above - Take photo from top-down perspective
  • Keep camera parallel to the drawer surface

Lighting & Environment

  • Use even lighting - Flashlight or overhead light recommended
  • Minimize shadows - Avoid harsh directional lighting
  • Clean background - Use monotone, uncluttered surface around drawer
  • Remove reflective objects that might interfere with detection

Framing

  • Include entire drawer in the frame with some margin
  • Ensure all four corners of the drawer are clearly visible
  • Keep drawer edges straight - avoid tilted or angled shots
  • Fill most of the frame with the drawer for better accuracy

Image Quality

  • Use good resolution - Higher quality images work better
  • Keep image sharp - Avoid blurry or out-of-focus shots
  • Stable camera - Use tripod or steady hands to avoid motion blur

Tip: A well-lit, straight-down photo with clear drawer edges will give you the most accurate measurements!
